Drawing, painting, poetry, and collaging, are the mechanisms through which I generally choose to express myself and express how I perceive the world around me. In my artwork I see a glimpse of things that I had once seen before; things I had seen in the natural world, simply displayed through the different lens of visual art. Yet through my

I like to express humanity in its most true form, to take both the joy that comes with being human and the pain that comes with being human. I do not want to let any fragment of what it means to be human in this world be left behind, or underrepresented.

The mediums I use are the ones that come most inherently to me. The artistic choices I make are the ones that bring me the most joy.

Creating is a part of my being. My hope is that I not only represent humanity in its true form, but also stay true to myself. After all, every artwork I create is, in some form, a self-portrait; it shares a little bit of information about myself and how I view the world that surrounds me.
