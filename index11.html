Hello! I am a visual artist currently calling home the San Francisco Bay Area. I find myself most interested in painting, drawing, experimenting with colors, cutting up papers, ripping up papers, gluing up papers, and giving life to characters through illustration. You can catch me studying it up at San Jose State University where I am pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree, jamming out on my ukulele at an open mic, or hiking in the beautiful California redwoods.
